Blockchain, FinTech, & Cryptography Club at Arizona State University

Mission

Blockchain, Fintech & Cryptography Club is affiliated with the ASU Blockchain Research Lab. Dragan Boscovic, BFC’s current advisor, is also founder and director of the Blockchain Research Lab. The club and the lab will share resources to further the purposes of both organizations.

Educate

Educate ASU students about the computer science, cryptography, and underpinning technology and implications of blockchain, and about cryptocurrency markets, trading techniques, and storage.

Research  & Development

Devise, analyze and promote blockchain use in creative and diverse domains (like DeFi, healthcare, supply chain etc.) and advance the research and development of blockchain technologies.

Promote

Create a sustainable, lasting community of blockchain enthusiasts that share ideas, resources, and knowledge with members and nonmembers alike.
